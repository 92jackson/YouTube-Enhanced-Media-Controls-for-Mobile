YouTube Behaviors

Autoplay
  • Default - No changes to initial page load
  • Attempt Unmuted Autoplay - Unmutes the video and closes the large play button overlay to prepare for autoplay (your browser may still pause the video automatically).
Auto-dismiss Interruptions
Automatically dismisses "Continue Watching?" and "Ready to shop?" dialogs.
Fix Stuck Playlists
Automatically reloads playlists that get stuck on loading.
Background Playback
Keeps YouTube playing while using other tabs, apps, or with the screen off. Note: Most browsers may still pause the video, but you can resume playback from your notification controls.
Playlist-Only Media Keys
Prevents media keys (Android notifications, Bluetooth) from skipping outside the current playlist to suggested videos.
Hide Video Player
Hides the video player to free up space for metadata, comments, and suggested videos. Ideal for music or podcast playback. This could help with the legality of using YouTube as a music player in your vehicle, as some regions prohibit the display of video visible to the driver.
Fixed Video Height
Sets the YouTube video element to a fixed height of 30% of the screen height.
Auto-Pause on Buffering
Automatically pauses the video when repeated buffering is detected to help with slow connections. Most browsers handle this automatically, but sometimes not reliably on slow connections.
Buffer Events Required
The number of buffering events within the threshold window required to trigger auto-pause. For example, 1 triggers on the first event, while 3 triggers on the third.
Buffering Detection Threshold
Time between buffering events to trigger auto-pause. Default: 3 seconds.
Minimum Buffer Duration
Minimum duration a buffer event must last to be counted. Prevents micro-buffer events from triggering auto-pause. Default: 1 second.
Auto-Pause Duration
How long to pause for when triggered. Default: 5 seconds.
