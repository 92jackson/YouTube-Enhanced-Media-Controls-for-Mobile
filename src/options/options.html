Behavior

Automatically extracts artist and song names from video titles (e.g., "Artist - Song Title") and displays them cleanly instead of showing the channel name.

• 'Disabled' - Show original video and channel names
• 'Mixes Only' - Extract names from YouTube-generated mixes
• 'Mixes and Playlists' - Extract from mixes and your playlists
• 'Always Parse' - Extract from all videos (may affect non-music content)

Remove Duplicate Titles
Prevents multiple videos with the same title from appearing in playlists (useful for music videos).
Allow Different Versions
Useful for allowing different versions of the same music video (remixes for example).

Background Playback
Keeps YouTube playing while using other tabs, apps, or with the screen off. Note: Most browsers will still pause the video, but you can resume playback from your notification controls.
Playlist-Only Media Keys Fix
Prevents media keys (Android notifications, Bluetooth) from skipping outside the current playlist to suggested videos.
Hide Video Player
Hides the video player to free up space for metadata, comments, and suggested videos. Ideal for music or podcast playback. This could help with the legality of using YouTube as a music player in your vehicle, as some regions prohibit the display of video visible to the driver.
Auto-Pause on Buffering Fix Experimental
Automatically pauses the video when repeated buffering is detected to help with slow connections. Most browsers handle this automatically, but some do not do it reliably.
Time between buffering events to trigger auto-pause. Default: 3 seconds.
How long to pause for when triggered. Default: 5 seconds.
Number of buffer events within the threshold window required to trigger auto-pause. 1 = trigger on first event, 3 = trigger on third.
